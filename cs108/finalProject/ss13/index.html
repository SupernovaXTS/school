Space Station 13 (SS13)

Overview

Space Station 13 or SS13 is a conflict driven multiplayer role-playing game set on a space station.

Players take various roles to either maintain the station or attempt to sabotage it.

History

For a detailed history of SS13, please visit the History Page.

Features

  • Role-playing as different crew members
  • Complex systems for engineering, medical, security, and more
  • Player driven narratives
  • Extensive diversity

Departments

SS13 features various departments that players can join, each with unique roles and responsibilities:

  • Command
  • Engineering
  • Medical
  • Security
  • Science
  • Cargo
  • Service
