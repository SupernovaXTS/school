Space Station 13 - Security Department

Welcome to the Security Department page of my CS108 Final Project!

Overview

The Security Department in Space Station 13 is responsible for maintaining law and order on the station.

Security roles include:

  • Head of Security: Oversees all security operations and manages the security team.
  • Warden: Supervises the brig and manages prisoners.
  • Brig Physician: Provides medical care to prisoners and staff in the brig.
  • Security Officer: Patrols the station, enforces laws, and responds to incidents.
  • Security Assistant: Supports security officers and helps maintain order.
  • Detective: Investigates crimes and gathers evidence.
