Space Station 13 - Cargo Department

Welcome to the Cargo Department page of my CS108 Final Project!

Overview

The Cargo Department in Space Station 13 is responsible for managing the station's supplies and logistics.

Cargo roles include:

  • Quartermaster: Oversees cargo operations and manages orders.
  • Cargo Technician: Handles the loading and unloading of supplies.
  • Miner: Aquires resources for the station.
  • Explorer: Ventures into space for exploration.
