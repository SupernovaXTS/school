SS13 - Engineering Department

Welcome to the Engineering Department page of my CS108 Final Project!

Overview

Engineering is responsible for:

  • Maintaining power including solars and generators
  • Maintaining atmospherics.
  • Repairing the station.
  • Maintaining communications equipment

Roles in engineering include:

  • Chief Engineer: Oversees all engineering tasks and manages the engineering team.
  • Station Engineer: Handles repairs and maintenance of engineering systems.
  • Atmospheric Technician: Maintains atmospherics.
  • Signal Technician: Maintains communication systems.
