SS13 - Command Department

Welcome to the Command Department page of my CS108 Final Project!

Overview

Command acts as the head of the station, serving to coordinate the station.

Key roles within the Command Department include

  • Captain: Head of the station
  • Head of Personnel: Manages crew assignments, leads the service and supply departments
  • Chief Engineer: Oversees engineering
  • Chief Medical Officer: Leader of medical staff
  • Head of Security: Enforces space law
  • Research Director: Leads research
