Space Station 13 - Medical Department

Medical is responsible for:

  • Healing Injuries
  • Curing Diseases
  • Performing Surgeries
  • Performing Chemistry

Roles in medical include:

  • Chief Medical Officer: Oversees the medical department.
  • Paramedic: Rescues patients.
  • Medical Doctor: Diagnoses and treats patients.
  • Pathologist: Studies diseases and performs autopsies.
  • Chemist: Prepares medications and chemical treatments.
