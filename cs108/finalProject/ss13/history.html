History of Space Station 13 (SS13)

History

SS13 was originally developed by Exadv1, beginning as a atmospherics simulator

It has since evolved into a complex multiplayer game with a rich lore and diverse gameplay mechanics.

Key milestones in SS13's development include:

x

Goonstation History

  • 2000: BYOND platform launched
  • 2003: Initial development of SS13 begins
  • 2008: SS13 source code open sourced
  • 2008-2010: Goonstation becomes a prominent SS13 fork
  • 2010: Baystation and TGStation forks created from released gooncode
  • 2016: Goonstation source leaked by 0xCSRF
  • Feb 12 2020: Goonstation Code Leaked by disgruntled developer
  • April 2020: Goonstation open sources codebase excluding secret content

TGStation History

  • 2010: TGStation forked from Gooncode
  • 2013: First hosting crisis
  • 2014: Transition of power to a new admin team
  • 2015-2016: New host, TG becomes crowdfunded

SsethTide History

  • March 17 2019: SsethTzeentach creates a video that causes a surge in ss13's playerbase
  • 2020: TG-Station adds multi-z support, box station removed
  • 2021: TG-Station refactors intents into combat mode
